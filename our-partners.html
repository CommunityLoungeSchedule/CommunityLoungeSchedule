Our Partners

Meet the Organisations We Work With

Our mission is made possible through strong partnerships with dedicated organisations. Each partner brings unique expertise to support the community.

Wavemaker

Wavemaker is a leading provider of digital skills training, dedicated to making technology accessible and empowering people and communities.

Visit Wavemaker's Website

JET and Jet-ability

Run by Stoke-on-Trent City Council, the Jobs, Enterprise and Training (JET) service helps residents find employment, with tailored support for individuals with learning disabilities or autism.

Visit the JET Information Page

Step On

An NHS service that delivers support to people experiencing mental health difficulties, helping them find and maintain meaningful paid employment.

Visit the NHS Step On Page

Beam

A social enterprise that supports people experiencing homelessness to find and sustain housing and employment through crowdfunding and one-to-one support.

Visit Beam's Website
