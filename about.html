About The Inclusive Employability Hub

Our Programme

The Inclusive Employability Hub is a team of employability organisations working together to help people from all backgrounds and abilities find jobs, keep their jobs, and do great work! We’re like a one-stop shop for everything you need to succeed in your career.

What We Offer

  • We’ll find helpful programmes and services in our area to support your job search.
  • You will be assigned a dedicated key worker to understand the challenges you face and create a plan to overcome them.
  • We can help you develop the basic skills you need to succeed in your workplace.
  • Even after you find a job, we’ll offer support for the first 6 months to ensure a smooth transition.

Who We Help

The Inclusive Employability Hub helps people in many situations find and keep jobs, including those who:

  • Haven’t been working recently (economically inactive or long-term unemployed).
  • Have health problems or disabilities that make working trickier.
  • Are refugees who are new to the area and want to find work.
  • Are experiencing homelessness and want to get back on their feet.
